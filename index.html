How it works

The Transparency standard.

Every company we build or invest in must meet three requirements before launch.

01

Full cost disclosure

We publish a breakdown of what the product or service actually costs to deliver — materials, labor, platform overhead, logistics. No rounding. No vague "production costs."

02

Plain-language terms

Warranties, refund policies, and contracts written in one paragraph or less. If you need a lawyer to understand your purchase, we failed.

03

Revenue sharing visibility

For platform businesses — content, marketplaces, services — every stakeholder can see exactly what percentage goes where. Creators, partners, and customers all have the same information.
